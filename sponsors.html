Why sponsor a toaster?

An FTC team is a student-run engineering company with one product, a twelve-week deadline, and zero revenue. That's where you come in.

Sponsorship covers parts, registration, field elements, and travel, and they add up fast. In exchange you get logo placement, a documented season of community impact, and a crew of future engineers who remember who funded them. Every dollar goes straight into the machine (and occasionally the fire-extinguisher budget).

Where the money goes

Robot parts & spares: the big line item
Competition registration: non-negotiable
Field elements: plywood and precision
Travel: the robot rides shotgun
Team toast: pennies, enormous ROI
