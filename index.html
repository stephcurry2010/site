Built in a garage,
fueled by burnt toast.

The Underengineered Toasters are a student-run FIRST Tech Challenge team with a simple philosophy: keep it simple, keep it scrappy, and never stop iterating. We design, build, code, and drive competitive robots, and we have a blast doing it.

Community Outreach

We are dedicated to spreading STEM in our community by mentoring younger robotics students, running local demos, and hosting open garage build days.

Engineering & CAD

CAD-first design, custom fabrication, and a relentless build-test-break-rebuild loop to refine our designs.

Autonomous & Control

AprilTag localization, odometry-based navigation, and precise driver-controlled tele-op routines.
