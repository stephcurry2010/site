// who we are

Built in a garage,
fueled by burnt toast.

The Underengineered Toasters are a student-run FIRST Tech Challenge team with a simple philosophy: keep it simple, keep it scrappy, and never stop iterating. We design, build, code, and drive competitive robots — and we have a blast doing it.

🔧

Engineering

CAD, fabrication, and a relentless build-test-break-rebuild loop until the bot does exactly what we need.

💻

Programming

Autonomous routines, sensor fusion, and driver-controlled code written and tuned by our software crew.

🤝

Outreach

Spreading STEM in our community — mentoring younger students and running local robotics demos.
